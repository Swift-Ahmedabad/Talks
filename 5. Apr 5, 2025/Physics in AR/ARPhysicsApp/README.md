# AR Physics App

Drop and throw balls that bounce off real surfaces in your room! Use your phone's camera to scan your environment, then watch balls interact with walls, floors, and furniture.

## What You Need

- iPhone 12 Pro or newer (with LiDAR sensor)
- iPad Pro (2020 or later)

## How to Use

### Step 1: Scan Your Room

1. Open the app
2. Slowly move your phone around pointing at:
   - Floor
   - Walls
   - Furniture
3. You'll see a colorful mesh appear showing what's been scanned

**Tips:**
- Move slowly for best results
- Make sure the room has good lighting
- Get close to surfaces (about arm's length away)
